A PERN stack app designed to mimic commercial approaches to case management. This particular app simulates healthcare appeal cases. Utilizes PostgreSQL as a database, Node.js and Express.js as backend and served via React.js. App enables authorization via a third party service, Keycloak, however that is currently disabled to allow unauthorized access.
